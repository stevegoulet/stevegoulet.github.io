Why We Exist

We are a team of doctors and psychologists who believe that a certified Addiction diagnosis and treatment should not be complicated or out of reach. We created Addiction Online to streamline the process of diagnosis and treatment. Our network of licensed psychologists can provide a certified diagnosis and our network of physicians stand by to provide treatment plans and medication management.

In short,our missionis to be here for you every step of the way.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you accept insurance?

A: While we do not accept insurance, our services are reimbursed up to 100% by most insurance plans. You can also use your HSA and FSA accounts.

Q: How much does it cost?

A: The max out-of-pocket you will spend on our Austism assessment is $1,499.

Q: How does the assessment work?

A: Our proprietary, dynamic, and logic-based assessment alters questions as you go based on the answers you provide to ensure our network of psychologists have all the information they need to make or exclude an Addiction diagnosis.

Q: Will I get to see my results?

A: We believe in transparency. You will receive a full copy of your or your childs records.

Q: Is this process confidential?

A: We are 100% safe and confidential with your information, using bank-level encryption. We are fully HIPPA compliant.

We never share your information with any third party unless you specifically request we do so in writing. The information you share is protected by the same doctor-patient privilege you would expect in an office visit.
